Q. How long does shipping take?

A. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. We ship through USPS, and generally orders take from 3-7 days to ship. That number varies for international orders.

For more shipping questions, email us at woodshoprva@gmail.com.

Q. Where does Wood Shop ship?

A. Currently we ship everywhere internationally!

What happens if I put in the wrong address?

A. We are not responsible for anything that happens after dropping off your package at the post office.

If you put in the wrong address and don't let us know before we create a shipping label and send it off, your package will be sent back to us and we will have to refund you for your order, however you will not be refunded for shipping costs.

Q. How should I wash my shirt/crewneck?

A. We recommend washing inside-out on the cold setting and hanging to dry.
